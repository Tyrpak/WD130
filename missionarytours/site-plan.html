Overview

Purpose

I live in Budapest, the capital of Hungary, which is a beautiful European city. The missionaries serving here only have a few hours time on Monday Preparation Days. So I have selected some routes that show them some of the must-see places in the city in that small time.

Audience

The missionaries can check out the routes and pick the one they like / where they haven't been yet.
