Route #1 - Pest Tour

A short walk through the major sites of Pest.

Batthyány tér -> Margit-híd -> Parlament -> Danube bank -> optional detour to Bazilika -> Danube bank -> Zikkurat

  • Duration: 3-5 hours
  • Length: 5 kilometers walking + 3 kilometers with tram or walking
  • Starting point: Batthyány tér
  • Ending point: Zikkurat at Lágymányosi híd

 

Starting Point: Batthyány tér

Best photo opportunity with the Parlament in the background. We take a walk to Margit-híd, and we can see the Pest side along the way, where we will be going next.
The photo is an illustration, and does not contain smiling missionaries.

 

Waypoint #1: Margit-híd

We cross the Danube on Margit-híd, where you can take pictures down river of a dozen of historic sights in Budapest.
You can also watch the half kilometer (quarter mile) wide river.

 

Waypoint #2: Dunapart

From the Pest bank of Margit-híd, we go around the majestic building of the Parlament, visiting Kossuth tér, and back to the river again.
We watch the Holocaust memorial of the bronze shoes. We can go down to the water here.

 

Optional Waypoint: Bazilika

There is a street running from the riverside to the Bazilika, which is a nice photo opportunity, with either the Buda castle or the Bazilika as a background.
The Bazilika is Hungary's biggest cathedral, which houses the right hand of Saint-Stephen.

 

Waypoint #3: Dunapart again

The promenade on the Pest bank offers a wonderful view of the Buda castle and Gellért-hegy. It's called Korzó, from the italian word "corso", which means course.
There is also a Starbucks, with a view, hot chocolate and a bathroom...

 

Ending Point: Zikkurat at Lágymányosi Híd

From Erzsébet-híd we can take a few stops with tram line Nr. 2., or walk all along the river.
There are some interesting new buildings at the end of our tour, one is a small look-out tower in the shape of a zikkurat.
